## Welcome to Quilox

Quilox ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use the Quilox mobile application (the "App").

This policy complies with the **Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019** and international data protection standards.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

When you use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, date of birth
- **Booking Information:** Guest name, contact details, table preferences, special requests
- **Payment Information:** Payment card details (processed securely by third-party payment providers)
- **Profile Information:** Profile photo, preferences, booking history

### 1.2 Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent in the App

- **Log Data:** IP address, access times, app crashes, performance data

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:
- Social media platforms (if you choose to connect your account)
- Payment processors
- Analytics providers

## 4. Your Data Protection Rights (NDPR)

Under Nigerian law, you have the following rights:

### 4.1 Right to Access (Article 3.1.1)
You can request a copy of your personal data we hold.

### 4.2 Right to Rectification (Article 3.1.2)
You can correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

### 4.3 Right to Erasure (Article 3.1.3)
You can request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

### 4.4 Right to Data Portability (Article 3.1.4)
You can receive your data in a machine-readable format.

### 4.5 Right to Restrict Processing (Article 3.1.5)
You can request that we limit how we use your data.

### 4.6 Right to Object (Article 3.1.6)
You can object to certain types of processing.

### 4.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

**How to Exercise Your Rights:**

- **In-App:** Settings > Privacy > Data Rights
- **Phone:** +234-XXX-XXXX-XXXX

We will respond to your request within **30 days**.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary:

| Data Type | Retention Period | Reason |
|-----------|------------------|--------|
| Account Information | 2 years after last activity | Service provision |
| Booking History | 2 years after booking | Records and support |
| Payment Records | 7 years | Tax and legal requirements |
| Technical Logs | 90 days | Security and troubleshooting |
| Marketing Consent | 2 years or until withdrawn | NDPR compliance |

After the retention period, we will securely delete or anonymize your data.

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

### 6.1 Technical Measures

- **Encryption in Transit:** TLS 1.3 encryption for all data transmission
- **Encryption at Rest:** AES-256 encryption for stored data
- **Secure Storage:** Hardware-backed encryption on mobile devices
  - iOS: Keychain (AES-256)
  - Android: KeyStore System (AES-256, TEE)

### 6.2 Organizational Measures

- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits and assessments
- Employee training on data protection
- Incident response procedures

### 6.3 ISO 27001 Compliance

Our information security practices comply with ISO/IEC 27001:2013 standards.
